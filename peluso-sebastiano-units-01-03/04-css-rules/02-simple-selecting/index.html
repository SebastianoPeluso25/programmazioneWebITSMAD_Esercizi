Torino

Turin is a city located in the northwest of Italy, in the heart of the Piedmont region. Known for its elegance, it is one of the most fascinating and historic cities in the country. Turin is famous for its baroque and neoclassical architecture, with large squares, historic buildings and numerous museums, including the Egyptian Museum, which houses one of the most important collections of Egyptian art in the world.

The city is also famous for its connection with the automobile, being the headquarters of Fiat, and for its gastronomic tradition, which includes the famous chocolate and gianduia. Turin is the home of the "bicerin", a hot drink made with coffee, chocolate and milk cream, and vermouth, a very popular aperitif.

From a cultural point of view, Turin is a city of great importance, with a lively theater and music scene. It hosts numerous international events, including the Turin Film Festival, and has a strong connection with classical music, thanks to the Teatro Regio.

Milano

Milan is one of the most dynamic and cosmopolitan cities in Italy, located in the Lombardy region in the north of the country. It is known as the economic and financial capital of Italy, as well as a world-famous center for fashion, design and art.

Milan is famous for its modern skyline, characterized by innovative skyscrapers such as those of the Porta Nuova district and the Unicredit Tower, which stand alongside the historicity of its monuments. The Milan Cathedral, one of the largest and most impressive Gothic cathedrals in Europe, is the symbol of the city and one of the major tourist attractions. A few steps from the Cathedral is the Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II, an elegant shopping mall that combines shopping and historic architecture. Milan is the heart of international fashion, home to the most important fashion houses and hosts the famous "Fashion Week", an event that attracts designers and celebrities from all over the world. The city is also a point of reference for design, with events such as the Salone del Mobile that every year brings the best of the creativity and innovation of the sector to Milan.
